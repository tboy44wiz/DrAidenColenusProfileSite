Westernization

The westernization of the eyelids is one of the plastic surgeries most sought after by young orientals, as some of them have a smooth upper eyelid without folds. But those who think that it is performed only for aesthetic reasons are wrong, since over the years excess skin can be harmful to vision. How it is: "under local anesthesia associated with light sedation, the plastic surgeon makes an incision just above the eyelashes and in the epicanthus (the region where the upper and lower eyelids meet, close to the nose) where the supratarsal groove is created (the crease above the eyelashes) and the fold in the epicanthus was corrected", explains plastic surgeon Aiden Colenus (RJ). Immediately after the surgery, the upper eyelid with western features is noticed naturally, but it becomes more evident as the edema disappears."
